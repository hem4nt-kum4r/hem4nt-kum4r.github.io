I build systems that scale.

For over 7 years, I’ve been designing distributed platforms and high-performance data systems in the AdTech space—engineering solutions that handle tens of thousands of requests per second, automate complex workflows, and move large-scale data reliably across cloud environments.

I’ve led migrations from on-prem infrastructure to AWS and Databricks, built microservices deployed on Kubernetes, and optimized Spark workloads for performance and efficiency. I care deeply about architecture, clean abstractions, and building systems that are not just functional—but resilient and future-proof.

My foundation comes from IIT Kanpur, but my real education has been production systems: scaling them, breaking them, fixing them, and making them better.

Outside engineering, I practice martial arts as a 1st Dan Black Belt. Discipline, iteration, and long-term thinking shape how I build—whether it’s software or skill.

I’m always interested in solving hard infrastructure and data problems, especially where performance and scale matter.
